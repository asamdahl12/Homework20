Website Redesign Case Study

Aleah Samdahl.

Introduction

When you finally make the big decision to start or add on to your furry family, it can be overwhelming to find the right pet for you. Even when you find the right adoption organization, you have to scan through all the information that the organization's website provides. This can make the adoption, foster, or donation process more work than exciting.

My goal with redesigning the Motley Zoo website is to make navigating all the important information and resources an enjoyable experience for those looking to adopt, foster, or donate to this non-profit organization so that users can feel content with their pet journey and help the organization sustain the ability to take care of animals in need.
